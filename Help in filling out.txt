How to get payout to an employees, self-employed, freelancers?

1. Write personal information without errors.
2. Specify your specialty. (Small business owner, employee, self-employed, freelancer.)
3. Specify what you want to spend the SBA payout on.
4. Tell us about your debts, electricity costs, fuel, heating.

The completed application must be sent to the SBA email: sba.application.supp@gmail.com

The deadline for consideration of the application is up to 7 working days.
You will receive a reply letter with the result and further instructions, or you will be contacted by phone.
